## Slide 5: Workflow
**From Ticket to Resolution — Streamlined**

1. **Ticket Assigned** → Dossier auto-populates
2. **Customer Info** → Office, device, history visible
3. **Assess Priority** → Recurring issue? Similar tickets?
4. **Contact Attorney** → Email, Teams, or Zoom
5. **Fix Steps** → Check off attempted solutions
6. **AI Assist** → Gemini suggests next steps
7. **Document** → Work notes auto-captured
8. **Resolve** → Close ticket, create KB if needed

**Result:** Faster resolution, consistent documentation, happy attorneys
