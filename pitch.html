The Problem: Death by a Thousand Tabs

When a technician gets assigned a ticket, they currently have to hunt across 4+ different systems just to understand what's happening.

  • 1 Open ServiceNow — read the ticket
  • 2 Check Entra/Intune — find the user's device
  • 3 Search old tickets — "has this happened before?"
  • 4 Hunt KB articles — "how do I fix this again?"
  • 5 Message user in Teams — "what office are you in?"
⏱️ Average lookup time: 8-12 minutes
😤 User frustration: High
❌ Missed context: Common
🔄 Repeated fixes: Every week
📉 SLA pressure: Always

The Solution: Ticket Dossier

A unified, intelligent dashboard that pulls everything a technician needs into a single view — automatically.

👤

Customer Profile

Name, title, office, phone, email, department, device, OS — all at a glance.

📋

Ticket Intelligence

Priority, SLA countdown, category, subcategory, assignment history — no surprises.

🔍

Similar Tickets

Auto-finds recent tickets with the same issue, same user, or same location.

📚

Relevant KB Articles

AI-matched knowledge base articles ranked by relevance to the current issue.

✅

Guided Fix Steps

Step-by-step troubleshooting pulled from KBs and past resolutions.

📊

Customer History

Timeline of the user's past tickets — spot patterns and recurring issues instantly.

Key Features

  • ⚡
    Instant Dossier Generation
    When a ticket is assigned, the dossier auto-populates in under 2 seconds.
  • 🎯
    Smart Similar Ticket Matching
    Uses natural language processing to find related issues, not just keyword matches.
  • 📱
    Tablet-Optimized for Field Work
    Designed for the Dell Latitude 7350 touchscreen in tablet mode. Big buttons, swipe-friendly, works offline.
  • 🤖
    AI-Powered Step Recommendations
    Suggests fix steps based on past successful resolutions and KB articles.
  • 🔔
    SLA & Escalation Alerts
    Real-time countdown and color-coded urgency so nothing slips through the cracks.

Everything in one view. No tabs. No hunting.
