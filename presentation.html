The Problem: Fragmented Information

  • Multiple systems to check (ServiceNow, SharePoint, Teams)
  • Customer info scattered across platforms
  • No visibility into recurring issues
  • Time wasted searching for basic details
  • Inconsistent service experience

Result: Slow resolution, frustrated attorneys, reactive support

The Solution: Unified Intelligence Dashboard

A single-view interface pulling everything together:

  • ServiceNow ticket details
  • SharePoint directory data
  • Customer history & device info
  • Knowledge base articles
  • AI-powered troubleshooting (Gemini)

All in one place. All at a glance.

Key Features

Customer Intelligence

  • Office & phone
  • Device assignments
  • Teams status & last login
  • 5-ticket history

Smart Troubleshooting

  • Interactive fix steps
  • AI suggestions from Gemini
  • Similar ticket matching
  • One-click KB creation

Workflow: From Ticket to Resolution

1. Ticket Assigned — Dossier auto-populates

2. Customer Info — Office, device, history visible

3. Assess Priority — Recurring? Similar tickets?

4. Check Availability — Location (Remote/In Office) + Teams status

5. Contact Attorney — In person, email, Teams, or Zoom

6. Fix Steps — Check off attempted solutions

7. AI Assist — Gemini suggests next steps

8. Document — Work notes auto-captured

9. Resolve — Close ticket, create KB
